About Us

Baitul Steel is a fabrication company in its fabrication or manufacturing process including receiving, cutting, flaging, pressing, rolling, assembling, welding, NDT, painting, packaging and shipping. We offer cooperation related to pressure vessels. Our specialists in pressure vessel work are intended for the oil and gas, chemical, and natural gas storage industries. We balance materials, technology, and staff to produce superior baitul steel products.
